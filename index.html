Built for cats.
Designed for you.

Every feature is purpose-built around one goal: making sure your cat always has a fresh, clean space.

VOC & Ammonia Monitoring

A metal-oxide sensor continuously measures volatile organic compounds and ammonia build-up — the invisible signs of a dirty litter box.

Smart Clean / Dirty Status

Sensor readings are automatically interpreted as Clean or Dirty. No thresholds to configure — it just works. The status updates in real time.

Camera Snapshots

A visual log of your litter box over time. Snapshots are taken automatically and stored so you can review what changed and when.

Detailed Sensor Log

Browse the full history of VOC readings with timestamps. Spot patterns, see when peak levels occur, and optimise your cleaning schedule.

Multi-Device Support

Manage multiple litter boxes from a single app. Switch between devices instantly and give each one a custom name and icon.

Manual Tracking Mode

No sensor? No problem. Use manual mode to log cleaning sessions by hand. LitterCheck will track timing and remind you when it's time.

How it works

From sensor
to status.

1
Sensor reads the air

The BME680 sensor inside the litter box enclosure measures VOC levels continuously. Readings are pushed to Firebase in real time.

2
App interprets the data

LitterCheck analyses VOC trends to determine whether the litter box is clean or dirty, and surfaces the status front and centre.

3
You clean. You confirm.

Once cleaned, tap "Mark as Cleaned" in the app. The status resets, and the cycle continues — with a grace period based on your cat count.
