01

Overview

LitterCheck ("we", "us", or "our") is a mobile application that connects to smart litter box sensors to monitor air quality (VOC/ammonia levels) and provide real-time cleanliness status for your cat's litter box.

This Privacy Policy explains what information LitterCheck collects, how it is used, and how it is protected. By using LitterCheck, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

The short version: LitterCheck collects only the data necessary to make the app work — sensor readings from your device, and local preferences you set. We do not sell your data, and we do not collect personal information beyond what you choose to provide when seeking support.

02

Information We Collect

LitterCheck collects the following categories of information:

Category What it includes Where it's stored
Sensor data VOC (volatile organic compound) readings, IAQ index, timestamps, inferred litter box status (Clean/Dirty) Firebase Realtime Database
Camera snapshots Images captured by the connected hardware camera module Firebase Realtime Database
Device profiles Device IDs, custom names, icons, cat count — set by you On-device (shared preferences)
Cleaning history Timestamps of when you marked the litter box as cleaned On-device (shared preferences)
App preferences Dark/light mode setting, active device selection On-device (shared preferences)

We do not collect:

  • Your name, email address, or any personal identifiers (unless you contact us for support)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or calendar access
  • Payment or financial information
  • Health or biometric data about you or your household members
03

How We Use Your Information

The data LitterCheck collects is used solely to provide and improve the app's core functionality:

  • Display real-time litter box status — sensor readings are fetched and analysed to show whether your litter box is Clean or Dirty.
  • Show historical logs — past sensor readings and camera snapshots are retrieved so you can review trends over time.
  • Track cleaning history — cleaning timestamps are stored locally so the app can calculate how long it has been since the last clean.
  • Provide cat health recommendations — based on the number of cats you enter, the app adjusts recommended cleaning intervals.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond delivering the app's intended features.

04

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for commercial purposes.

Sensor readings are stored in Google Firebase Realtime Database, which is accessible by the device that writes them (your hardware sensor) and the app you use to read them. Firebase operates under Google's privacy and security standards.

Data in Firebase is associated with your device ID (a unique hardware identifier for the sensor). There is no account or login required — access is controlled at the database rules level by device ID.

We may disclose information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal processes, but we have no mechanism to identify individual users without a support contact being initiated by the user themselves.

05

Data Storage & Security

LitterCheck uses Google Firebase Realtime Database for cloud storage of sensor data. Firebase is hosted on Google Cloud infrastructure and benefits from Google's enterprise-grade security measures, including encryption in transit (TLS) and at rest.

Local data — device profiles, preferences, and cleaning history — is stored using Flutter shared preferences, which is encrypted by the operating system's secure storage on both iOS and Android.

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by removing the app from your device. Sensor data in Firebase is associated with your device ID; to request deletion of Firebase data, contact us using the details in Section 10.

06

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your personal data under applicable privacy laws (including GDPR, CCPA, and similar regulations). These include:

  • Right of access — request a copy of the data we hold associated with your device.
  • Right to deletion — request that data associated with your device be permanently deleted from Firebase.
  • Right to portability — request an export of sensor data associated with your device.
  • Right to object — object to certain uses of your data.

Because LitterCheck does not collect account information, requests are handled by providing your device ID (found in the app's Settings page under your device profile). Please include it when contacting us.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address listed in Section 10.

07

Children's Privacy

LitterCheck is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the app, please contact us and we will take steps to delete that information.
